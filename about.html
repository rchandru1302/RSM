About Us

Dr. R.S. Jayalakshmi

Dr. R.S. Jayalakshmi is a revered figure in the world of Carnatic music, known for her lifelong dedication to training students in the classical tradition. Her teaching spans decades, with a special emphasis on mastering challenging aspects such as raga, laya, neraval, and svara.

She has mentored hundreds of students, many of whom have gone on to become performers and educators themselves. Her pedagogy is rooted in discipline, clarity, and deep musical insight.

Dr. Jayalakshmi has been honored with several prestigious awards, including the Sangita Kala Acharya, recognizing her exceptional contribution to music education.

Charulatha Chandrasekar

Charulatha Chandrasekar is a dynamic musician and educator, known for her versatility and precision in both vocal and veena performance. She has received accolades for her contributions to music education and concert organization.
