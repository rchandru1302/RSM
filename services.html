Training Services

Our institution offers rigorous and inspiring training in Vocal, Veena, and Mridangam, rooted in the rich traditions of Carnatic music. Students are guided through both Kalpita Sangita (composed music) and Manodharma Sangita (improvisational music), nurturing both discipline and creativity.

We emphasize the importance of Laya (rhythm), introducing students to a wide range of Talas that form the rhythmic backbone of Carnatic performance.

Rhythmic training is central to our Mridangam program, which builds both theoretical and practical mastery:

  • Understanding Laya and rendering Tala using hand gestures
  • Learning sollus: Tha, Thi, Thom, Nam, Chapu, etc.
  • Basic Talas: Adi Tala, Rupaka Tala, Misra Chapu, Kanda Chapu
  • Advanced Talas: Sapta Tala-s and beyond
  • Korvai construction and eduppu techniques across Talas
  • Precise hand and finger positioning for clarity and speed

Every student enrolling in these streams is in expert hands, guided by seasoned faculty and a legacy of excellence in Carnatic music education.
